About Me

I am a theoretical and computational chemist specializing in density functional theory (DFT), time-dependent DFT, and electronic-structure methods for excited and metastable states. I am currently a research fellow at Northwestern University.

Research Interests

  • Auto-ionizing and Resonance States (Non-Hermitian Methods)
  • Negative Ion Resonances and CAP-based Approaches
  • Excited-State Potential Energy Surfaces
  • Method Development and High-Performance Scientific Computing

I am a physical chemist specializing in quantum-chemistry approaches to chemical problems. In the spirit of the uncertainty principle, I am currently located at \color Northwestern University, with research momentum extending globally.My research focuses on the development of new theoretical frameworks, computational methodologies, and efficient algorithms to address fundamental and applied challenges in chemistry, with the aim of achieving experimental-level chemical accuracy.
